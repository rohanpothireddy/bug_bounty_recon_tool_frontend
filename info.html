INFO - OTHER USEFUL RESOURCE LINKS

USERNAME LOOKUP

Hey! Didn’t find what you are here for? Try these:

Webmii - Gives out information about a person available on the internet according to the name entered.

TweeterID - This website is useful in getting Twitter ID from @handle, which is a unique value that every account on Twitter has. No two people have the same ID. Although an account can change its @handle, it can never change its Twitter ID.

Lookup-ID.com - This website might be helpful in getting the Facebook numeric ID through username, sometimes helpful in finding profiles masked by other similar named profiles on alphabetical search.

WEBSITE LOOKUP

Hey! Didn’t find what you are here for? Try these:

WHOIS Domain Lookup - Find out who owns a website - GoDaddy CA - is a domain registrar and web hosting company that offers a WHOIS Domain Lookup service. WHOIS is a protocol that allows users to query databases and retrieve information about domain names.

Whois.com - allows users to search for domain information by entering the domain name, and it retrieves details such as the registrant's name, contact information, registration date, and domain status.

Internet Archive: Wayback Machine - is a digital archive of web pages and websites, allows users to browse and access archived versions of websites as they have appeared at different points in time.

IP-ADDRESS LOOKUP

Hey! Didn't find what you are here for? Try these:

IPAddress.com - allows users to look up information about an IP address, such as its location, owner, and associated domain names.

Myip.ms - has tools to discover the IP address and geolocation of a website or server, information about web hosting providers, including their IP ranges, company details, and server statistics.

IP Tracker - input an IP address and get its location, including city, country, latitude, and longitude. It may also provide additional details such as the internet service provider (ISP).

PHONE NUMBER LOOKUP

Hey! Didn't find what you are here for? Try these:

FreeCarrierLookup.com - provides information about the carrier or service provider associated with a given phone number.

Free lookup net - provides information about the carrier or service provider, current time in the timezone, and location associated with a given phone number.

EmobileTracker.Com - provides information about the carrier or service provider and timezone associated with a given phone number.

EMAIL LOOKUP

Hey! Didn't find what you are here for? Try these:

RocketReach - allows individuals and businesses to search for email addresses, providing users with access to an extensive database of professional contact information.

Simple Email Reputation - provides users with insights into the reputation of an email address by analyzing various factors such as spam reports, blacklists, and email deliverability data.

Have I Been Pwned - enter their email address or username on the website, and it checks if their credentials have been exposed in known data breaches.

OSINT framework focused on gathering information from free tools or resources. The intention is to help people find free OSINT resources.
